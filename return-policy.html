Overview

At Nexus Digital, we stand behind the quality of our work. This Return & Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds or credits may be issued for our digital services.

Because we provide professional digital services (not physical products), refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis in line with the terms below. We always aim for a fair and transparent resolution.

Our commitment: If we have not delivered what was agreed, we will make it right — through a revision, partial refund, or full refund depending on the situation.

Nature of Our Services

Nexus Digital provides professional digital services including but not limited to:

  • Web design & development
  • Mobile app development (iOS & Android)
  • Brand identity & graphic design
  • SEO, paid advertising & growth marketing
  • Social media management
  • AI & automation integration
  • Monthly retainer & consulting services

As these are custom, time-intensive professional services, they differ from physical products. Once work has commenced, the time and resources invested cannot be fully recovered, which is reflected in our refund terms below.

Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund under the following circumstances:

  • We fail to deliver the agreed scope of work within the agreed timeline (without a mutually agreed extension)
  • The final deliverable significantly deviates from the agreed brief or approved concept
  • You cancel before work has commenced on a paid milestone
  • A technical error results in a duplicate or incorrect charge
  • We are unable to complete the project due to circumstances on our end
Not eligible for refund: Change of mind after work has commenced, delays caused by client-side factors (late feedback, content, approvals), or dissatisfaction with a direction that was previously approved.

Refund Schedule

For project-based engagements, the following refund schedule applies based on project stage at the time of cancellation:

Project Stage Refund Amount Status
Before kickoff / work not started 100% of amount paid Full Refund
Discovery & strategy phase (0–25%) 75% of amount paid Partial Refund
Design / development in progress (25–50%) 50% of amount paid Partial Refund
More than 50% complete 25% of amount paid Limited Refund
Delivered & approved by client No refund Non-Refundable
Monthly retainer (current month) Pro-rated for unused days Pro-rated

Non-Refundable Items

The following are strictly non-refundable under all circumstances:

  • Domain registration and renewal fees
  • Third-party software licences purchased on your behalf
  • Paid advertising spend (Google Ads, Meta Ads, etc.) — these are paid directly to platforms
  • Stock photography, fonts, or asset licences procured for your project
  • Rush / priority fees where the expedited work was completed
  • Consultation or strategy sessions that have already taken place
  • Any work that has been signed off / approved by the client in writing

Cancellation Policy

Project-based work: You may cancel a project at any time by notifying us in writing. Refunds will be calculated per the schedule above based on the stage of completion at the time of cancellation.

Monthly retainers: Retainer agreements can be cancelled with a minimum of 30 days written notice before the next billing cycle. Cancellations made within the 30-day window will be charged for the following month in full.

Annual contracts: Cancellation of annual contracts before the contract end date may incur an early termination fee equivalent to 2 months’ retainer value.

All cancellations must be submitted in writing via email to hello@nexusdigital.io. Verbal or WhatsApp cancellations are not accepted as formal notice.

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at hello@nexusdigital.io with the subject line: "Refund Request — [Your Project Name]"
  • Include your full name, company name, invoice number, and reason for the request
  • Attach any relevant supporting documentation or correspondence
  • We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days
  • A senior team member will review and respond with a decision within 7 business days
We take every refund request seriously. Our goal is to resolve disputes fairly and quickly — most requests are resolved within 5 business days.

Refund Processing Timeline

Once a refund is approved, the amount will be processed as follows:

  • Bank transfer / NEFT / IMPS: 3–5 business days
  • Credit / debit card: 5–10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • UPI: 1–3 business days
  • International wire transfer: 7–14 business days

Refunds are issued to the original payment method only. If that method is no longer available, we will work with you to find an alternate solution.

Disputes

We genuinely want every client to be happy with our work. If you are dissatisfied with a deliverable, we strongly encourage you to raise the issue with us directly before initiating a chargeback or formal dispute.

In most cases, we can resolve the issue through revisions, scope adjustments, or a partial credit — far faster than a formal dispute process.

In the event of an unresolved dispute, both parties agree to attempt mediation in good faith before pursuing any legal remedies. This policy is governed by the laws of Rajasthan, India.

Before raising a chargeback with your bank, please email us. Chargebacks without prior notice may result in suspension of services and could affect your credit with us permanently.
