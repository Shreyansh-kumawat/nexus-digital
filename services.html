FAQ

Questions we hear
all the time.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A brand identity project typically takes 2–4 weeks. A full web application can range from 6–16 weeks. We always share a detailed timeline before kicking off.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Absolutely. We love working with founders from day one. We offer flexible packages for startups that need to move fast without breaking the bank.

Can I hire you for just one service?

Yes. While we offer full-service packages, you can absolutely engage us for a single deliverable — a logo, a website, a marketing campaign. No bundling required.

What does the onboarding process look like?

We start with a discovery call, then send a tailored proposal within 48 hours. Once approved, we kick off with a strategy workshop and assign a dedicated project manager.

How do you handle revisions?

All our packages include revision rounds (usually 2–3 per milestone). We use collaborative tools like Figma and Notion so feedback is always clear and fast to action.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes. We offer flexible retainer plans for ongoing maintenance, updates, and growth. Many of our clients have been with us for 3+ years.
