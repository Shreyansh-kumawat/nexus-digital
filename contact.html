FAQ

Questions we get
asked all the time.

Still not sure? Here are honest answers to the most common questions before working with us.

How quickly will you respond to my message?
We respond to every message within 24 business hours — usually within a few hours. If it’s urgent, you can WhatsApp us directly at +91 98765 43210 and we’ll reply immediately.
What’s the minimum budget you work with?
Our project-based work typically starts from ₹1,50,000 and monthly retainers from ₹75,000/month. We believe in transparent pricing — no hidden fees, ever. We’ll let you know upfront if we’re not the right fit for your budget.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. We work with clients across India, UAE, UK, Singapore, and Australia. We have remote-first processes, async communication systems, and experience handling cross-timezone projects without friction.
How long does a typical project take?
It depends on scope. A website redesign takes 3–5 weeks. A full brand identity project takes 4–6 weeks. A mobile app takes 8–16 weeks depending on complexity. We always define timelines clearly in the proposal — and we stick to them.
Will I have a dedicated point of contact?
Yes. Every client gets a dedicated Project Lead who’s your single point of contact throughout the engagement. No bouncing between account managers or explaining your brand from scratch every call.
What makes Nexus different from other agencies?
Most agencies separate strategy, design, and development into silos. We don’t. Our teams are fully integrated, which means faster decisions, fewer handoff errors, and work that actually makes business sense — not just work that looks good in a portfolio.
Do you offer ongoing support after the project is done?
Yes. We offer monthly maintenance retainers for all projects we build. Most clients choose to continue with us for growth, optimisation, and iteration work post-launch. Long-term partnerships are how we do our best work.
